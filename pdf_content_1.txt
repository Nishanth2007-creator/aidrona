AIdrona - Detailed Explanation with 
System Roles 
Introduction 
AIdrona is the AI-powered medical intelligence system inside our application. Its main 
purpose is to ensure that only medically eligible users are allowed to donate blood during 
emergencies. 
Why AIdrona is Needed 
Without AIdrona, the system would send emergency requests to all users, including those 
who are not medically fit. This can be dangerous and inefficient. With AIdrona, only safe and 
eligible donors receive alerts. 
What AIdrona Stores 
ò Medical conditions (e.g., diabetes, blood pressure) 
ò Medications currently being taken 
ò Doctor prescriptions (image or PDF) 
ò Hemoglobin level 
ò Last blood donation date 
How AIdrona Works 
Step 1: Data Storage 
Users upload their medical data into the system. 
 
Step 2: AI Analysis 
The AI checks hemoglobin levels, donation history, diseases, and medications. 
 
Step 3: Eligibility Decision 
The system classifies users as eligible, temporarily not eligible, or not eligible. 
 
Step 4: Fitness Score 
A score is generated to rank donors based on health and reliability. 
How It Works in Emergencies 
1. A user raises a blood request. 
2. The system finds nearby users. 

3. AIdrona analyzes each userÆs medical data. 
4. Only eligible users receive the emergency popup. 
Real-Life Example 
If a user has low hemoglobin or recently donated blood, AIdrona will block them from 
receiving alerts. A healthy user will receive the alert. 
Simple Analogy 
AIdrona acts like a doctor inside the app who checks every user before allowing them to 
donate. 
Role of Hospitals 
ò Hospitals register through controlled access. 
ò They update vaccine stock availability. 
ò They verify donors during donation. 
ò They mark donors as fit or unfit. 
ò They update vaccine dose records. 
Role of Blood Banks 
ò Blood banks register through admin access. 
ò They update blood availability by blood group. 
ò They act as backup when no donors respond. 
ò They provide real-time blood stock information. 
Importance 
ò Ensures safety 
ò Improves efficiency 
ò Speeds up response 
Conclusion 
AIdrona makes the system intelligent, safe, and effective by using AI to filter and select the 
right donors, while hospitals and blood banks ensure resource availability and verification.

Node types, colour coding, and key terms

Marks the start of the workflow or a user-initiated action

Automated step performed by AiDrona AI without user input

A branching point or a state that requires evaluation

A step where the requester selects the next path
Fallback Option
Paths available when the primary route is exhausted
Success / Confirmed
Positive outcome: donor confirmed or verification passed
Escalation
Final fallback: blood bank alerted and requester notified
Decision diamond
A yes / no branch point in the flow. Each exit is labelled Yes or No.
Dashed arrow
Loop-back path (e.g. extend range returns to contact scan).
Key Terms
Fitness Score
A computed 0-100 score for each donor based on hemoglobin, donation gap, reliability, and medical
disqualifiers. Donors are ranked by this score before popups are sent.
Contacts-First
The app pings only users who appear in the requester's phone contacts before opening the request to
strangers. This preserves trust and increases response rates.
Radius Expansion
If the contacts-first search yields no eligible donors, the search radius is increased and the contact list
is re-scanned at the wider distance.
Blood Bank Fallback If no individual donor accepts after the stranger popup, the nearest blood bank is automatically alerted
with the blood type and urgency level.
QR Verification
Each registered user has a QR code linking to their live donor profile. Doctors scan this on arrival to
verify blood type, hemoglobin, and fitness status in real time.
